03 · How it works

One change on the Master. Every Follower flagged. The owner applies or rejects — logged.

One Master, many projects · propagation is unidirectional

Before
Decision in a meeting↓ Noted in a document↓ Sent by email↓ Updated by hand, or missed
After
Master edited↓ Follower flagged↓ Owner reviews↓ Applies or rejects · logged

Propagate as flags, not silent updates · the owner decides

Status readable by every role, not only engineers

05 · Lessons learned

Requirements and designs should not come from the same person.

In the next feature I asked the CPO to take over the requirements while I translated them into design. See Use Case 2, Verification Assignment Matrix.

Designed pre AI. Today AI would speed up first drafts, but new domain discovery still needs a human asking the non expert questions: AI knows job roles generally, not how a specific company operates.
