05

Recommendations

  1. 01Resolve Model vs File Explorer. The biggest IA decision. It gates the sidebar and default view.
  2. 02Bridge v1 and v2 in search. Small to build, high in value.
  3. 03Standardize icon style. Mixed flat and filled icons weaken the grammar.
  4. 04Map the core workflows. Create, connect, review. Surfaces gaps a static audit misses.
  5. 05Extend the teaching tooltips. The strongest lever for learning through use.
06

Working with AI

AI synthesizes frameworks, the knowledge is not from AI. To use Miller's Law well, I read the source, not the summary.

AI gives basics faster, experts hold the real detail. SysML basics from AI. How an engineer works from Cauê.

AI expands scope fast, and differently per person. Clear problem, role and scope keep the output in bounds.
