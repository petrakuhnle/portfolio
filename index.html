02 — Founding work

Building a design practice from zero

Founding design · 0 to 1

Promoted onto the newly founded Product Team

VALISPACE

Built the design practice from zero, then worked directly with the CTO, CPO, PM, and Dev leadership. Introduced Figma, prototyping, and UX research methods — interviews, click-through tests, say-out-loud sessions, information architecture sorting — fitted to context and goal, never applied as a blanket process.

Approach

Show, don't tell, how to integrate UX

VALISPACE

Took the initiative in individual collaborations to get fast, visible results rather than argue for process, making judgment calls with no playbook to follow. That outsider's eye — asking questions insiders wouldn't think to ask — plus fast-built prototypes made the case for design faster than argument alone. Started a cross-functional UX guild, then created and hired the senior role that still owns this work at Altium today.
