02 · UX Founding Work

How I integrated UX in an engineering led startup

Four initiatives I started as the first designer, next to the feature work · Valispace, 2021–2022.

Initiative 01 · UX practice · Valispace 2021–2022

Show, don't tell

Founded the UX Guild for designers and developers, and introduced user interviews and prototype testing by doing them.

Initiative 02 · Consistency · Valispace 2021–2022

One look and feel

Design principles and one look and feel across product, API docs and marketing, in collaboration with the marketing team.

Initiative 03 · Leadership · Valispace 2021–2022

From UX to Product

The approach became company practice and I was promoted onto the new Product team. I later drafted the role and hired my successor, still at Altium 3.5 years later.

Initiative 04 · Accessibility · Valispace 2021–2022

Accessibility as essential

Flagged a low-contrast red–green diagram colour-blind users later reported. Argued WCAG-aware design as essential in technical software, not nice-to-have.
