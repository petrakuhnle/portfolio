05

Background

My through-line is curiosity about the interaction between humans and their environment. Four years at Apple supporting professional creative tools (Logic Pro, Final Cut Pro) showed me where expert software fails in real use. A psychology degree in perception trained me to read behaviour before screens. My interest is in how the brain works, how language versus non-language works in communication, and how we interact with the world around us. Technology changes; human complexity remains part of the design process. My work is focussed on understanding in depth, asking good questions, designing with precision, and staying open to the gap between plan, expectations, and real-world users — curious about where it breaks.
