05 · Lessons learned

A hi fi prototype is a discovery tool, not just a deliverable.

Its detail level surfaces the questions that define the MVP. Designing the small system that works beats designing the complete system that doesn't ship.

Post AI: I haven't yet fed 100s of requirements to AI to generate a prototype, and in this field data security would constrain it. What I know from continuous iteration cycles: without human oversight, scope creep and inconsistencies grow, and an AI generated prototype still needs testing with individual users.
