Requirement state and V&V status at a glance

03 · Domain standards first

Discovery here meant learning the conventions before drawing anything.

Review software already existed in the industry, so I reviewed similar tools engineers used to understand the conventions they expect, then interviewed users on where those tools fail their day to day. The feature had to fit the existing platform, not be a product of its own.

Views resolve conventions, user needs and constraints

04 · Constraints

  • Industry standards define what a review must record · no freedom to reinvent the vocabulary
  • Business constraints: integrate into the existing platform and data model
  • A project can involve 1000s of requirements and 100s of people

05 · Approach

Working directly with engineers, mapping logic from the user side.

Experts and I mapped the logic in the language of the user, not yet the logic of the code. Development took the user stories and designs as the starting point and kept the flexibility to find the best backend and frontend solution. Views and filters per role, and a dashboard for status at a glance, came from that mapping.

06 · Key decisions

  • Assign groups of reviewers, not only individuals · reviews repeat, so reduce the cost over a project, not over one task
  • Rating per object with the assignment logged · approver closes with a timestamp
  • Views per role: filter per method for specialists, per requirement set and status for decision makers

07 · Impact

  • Backend feedback: the logic was solid, new feedback was easy to integrate
  • Shipped 2021 · live in Altium 365 in 2026
  • Third of the three features that won the contract and led to my promotion

08 · Lessons learned

Involve experts and expert users early, design earlier still. What I'd add: a middle step of first code prototypes, feeding development constraints back into the designs before full implementation.

09 · Relevant post AI in UX design

Testing changes with a human in the loop is not the same as testing alone: focus shifts when another human guides attention or asks for edge cases in anticipation. AI is not yet good at that mirroring. And a structure that doesn't need rework still requires a 360 degree overview of different users' needs, which is discovery work, not generation work.
